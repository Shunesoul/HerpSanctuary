Not Certain when to perform maitenance?

Nothing to worry about! We've got you covered. Sometimes when a collection becomes so large it can be hard to manage all the water changes, supplement days, misting days, etc. That why you can create an account and enter in your collection with its own specific requirements and needs and you can always check back in for your daily reminder of what you need to do to make sure your animals are happy and healthy.

Why would someone need this?

Let's face it we as keepers can get busy and may forget things but we dont want our animals to ever suffer for that. We have to ensure that we always give them the best that we can as keepers or we are not doing enough for them. Sometimes you build a collection up into the hundreds then have a sudden shift in work schedule and you are needing to make sure that you are still keeping a consistent routine for your animals. Everyone have been there before. I want to ensure no keep ever worries did they need to do something on anyday. As all there collections specific needs on a day to day basis can be stored and reviewed in a easy to read remainder note.
